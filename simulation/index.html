SPECIFIC GRAVITY TEST ON BITUMEN

Objective:

To determine the specific gravity of the given sample of bitumen.


Apparatus used:

Specific Gravity Bottle, Weighing Balance, Distilled Water.


 Description Specific Gravity of Bitumen: is defined by BIS as the ratio of the mass of a given volume of the bituminous materials to the mass of an equal volume of water, the temperature of both being specified as 27oC.
STEP  1 

 Cleaned and dried Specific Gravity bottle is weighed along with stopper W1(g).

Weight of empty Specific Gravity bottle(W1) = _____g

STEP  2 

 Specific Gravity bottle is filled with distilled water and is placed in water bath maintained at 27°C.

STEP  3 

 The Specific Gravity bottle containing distilled water is now weighed W2(g).

Weight of empty Specific Gravity bottle + Water(W2) = _____g

STEP  4 

 The bituminous material is heated to a pouring temperature.

STEP  5 

 The Specific Gravity bottle containing bitumen is now weighed W3(g).

Weight of empty Specific Gravity bottle + Bitumen(W3) = _____g

STEP  6 

 The remaining space in the Specific Gravity bottle is filled with distilled water and stopper is placed.

STEP  7 

 The Specific Gravity bottle containing bitumen + distilled water is now weighed W4(g).

Weight of Specific Gravity bottle+ Bitumen + Water(W4)= _____g

STEP  8 

 Observation and Results.

Specific gravity of Bitumen = (W3-W1)⁄((W2-W4)-(W1-W3))
